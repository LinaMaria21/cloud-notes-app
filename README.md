Cloud-based app
 Students keep all their notes in one place because it makes easier to stay organized and manage school work without losing important information.

Features:
All Notes in one Place
Save and organize the notes by subject or topic so you can find them easily.
Access Anywhere
Use the app on any device, anytime, as long as you have internet.
Simple Organization

Keep the notes neat and easy to follow with a clear system.
Track Your Grades
Keep an eye on your grades, assignments, and progress.
Easy to Use
The app is simple and clear, so you can focus on studying.
Purpose

The goal of this app is
to help students stay organized and save time. It brings notes and grade tracking together in one simple tool.
